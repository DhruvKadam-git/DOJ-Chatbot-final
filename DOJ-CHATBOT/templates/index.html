About Us

The Department of Justice (DoJ) is committed to ensuring justice for all. Learn more about our mission, values, and the services we offer.

Our Mission

The Department of Justice works tirelessly to uphold the rule of law, protect citizens' rights, and deliver swift justice. Our commitment is to make legal services accessible to everyone, ensuring that justice is not just a concept but a reality for all.

With a focus on transparency, efficiency, and integrity, we aim to maintain the highest standards in the legal field. From eFiling and ePay services to live streaming of court cases, we are constantly innovating to serve you better.

Our Services

Explore the range of services offered by the Department of Justice, designed to make justice more accessible and transparent.

Case Management

Manage and monitor case progress with our comprehensive case management system. Stay informed about case updates, hearings, and judgments.

eFiling & ePay

File your cases online with ease and make secure payments through our eFiling and ePay services. Simplifying the legal process for you.

Live Court Streaming

Watch live streaming of court proceedings from the comfort of your home. Stay connected and informed about the justice system.
